About Us

Smart Garden Irrigation System Sdn Bhd (SGIS) is an internet of Things (IoT) company based in Cyberjaya, Selangor, Malaysia. We are an MTDC-funded company that is registered with the Ministry of Finance, NIOSH and an approved vendor of PETRONAS.

Smart farming can provide accessibility, quality, and affordability.

1. The smart farming technique uses fertigation, in which a calculated amount of fertiliser is injected into the water, to fertilise plants. This has increased farmer output and decreased the cost of fertiliser used per plant. It also requires less labour.

2. The technology also aids farmers in interpreting the data they have gathered, paying close attention to the needs and problems unique to each crop. This aids in the quickest and most shrewd approach possible for farmers to obtain maximum production of healthy crops.

3. The system will appropriately prepare for the rotation of harvests and take into account each plant's needs, farmers will be able to avoid unpredictable harvests.
