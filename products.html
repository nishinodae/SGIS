Our Product

Product Overview

Our product is an IoT project specializing in home agriculture, which is Smart Garden Irrigation System (SGIS).The plant irrigation system are interconnected through the internet, and the user can monitor and control the irrigiation system from a mobile application. The system is equipped with sensors, actuators and mobile application.

By integrating IoT into a home garden irrigation system, SGIS will allow the home gardeners to remotely monitor the plants and control the irrigation system through their phones remotely.

Features and Benefits

Instantly Notified

Instant notifications about your home garden, such as low soil moisture, burst pipes or even clogged filters.

Remote Control

Control irrigation and fertilization from any location, at any time you want. Certainly save time on checking your garden daily.

Real-Time Analytics

Right decisions can be made because of real-time and historical irrigation data from the soil moisture and temperature sensors.

Software Products

Using our mobile app, BLOOM allows you to see your entire home garden anywhere and use an easy dashboard to monitor and control pumps, irrigation and much more.

Hardware Product

Smart Irrigation System

This product is able to monitor the condition of the plants by recording the humidity and moisture level of the soil and the surrounding temperature. Based on these parameters, the irrigation system automatically will irrigate the garden.

Automated Fertilizing System

By monitoring the content of nutrients of the plants using sensors, users are able to fertilize their plants through the mobile app.
